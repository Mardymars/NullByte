# NullByte Privacy Statement

NullByte is designed to work locally on your Android device. The app is a paid utility, not an account-based or cloud service.

## What NullByte does

- Imports media that you pick yourself or share into the app
- Checks supported files for common location, device, time, and author details
- Creates fresh clean copies of supported media formats
- Compares the original file to the clean copy so you can review what changed

## What NullByte does not do

- Does not declare an Internet permission
- Does not upload files to a server
- Does not create an account
- Does not collect analytics
- Does not show ads
- Does not process payment details inside the app
- Does not overwrite or delete your original files
- Does not silently monitor every download or file on your device

## Notifications

Notifications and reminders are optional. If enabled, they are used only for local reminders and local result messages on the device.

## Verification scope

NullByte verifies the common location, device, time, and author details it checks. A "verified clean" result means those checked details were not found after cleaning.

Some file formats can store unusual hidden details, so review sensitive files before sharing. Video and audio support is format-specific: NullByte supports MP4, MOV, 3GP, M4A, MP3, and WAV files.

## Purchase scope

The app store handles the purchase. NullByte does not require an in-app account, subscription, analytics profile, or cloud processing service to use the core cleaning flow.
