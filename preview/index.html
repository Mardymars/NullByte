What NullByte does

NullByte creates a fresh share-ready copy of your photo without the common metadata that can reveal more than you intended.

  • Scans common GPS, camera, time, and author-style tags locally.
  • Explains the flow in simple language before the user does anything.

  2. Review the local scan summary for common EXIF tags.
